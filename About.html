ABOUT US

Founded by Mr. K. Balasubramanian, we are a trusted manufacturer and retailer of high-quality machinery and industrial tools. Since 2019, our skilled team has been committed to delivering durable, efficient, and value-driven solutions for both retail and wholesale clients. We take pride in our precision, performance, and reliability, helping industries achieve lasting results.

Coffee Machines – Efficient and durable machines built for consistent roasting, grinding, and brewing performance. Rice Mill Machines – Advanced milling solutions for smooth processing and high yield with minimal wastage. Vibro Sifters – High-speed, precision sifters designed for uniform separation and fine material grading. Paddy Separators – Reliable machines that ensure accurate grain separation and improved processing efficiency. Pre Cleaners – Sturdy, rotary-type cleaners that remove impurities to enhance grain quality before processing. Our machines are trusted by industries for their high efficiency, low maintenance, and long-lasting performance, making us a preferred choice for quality machinery solutions.
